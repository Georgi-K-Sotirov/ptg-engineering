About Us

PTG Engineering Ltd. is a specialized company in electrical installations and industrial solutions. We offer a full range of services – from electrical system design and installation to building main distribution boards, control panels and automated equipment management systems.

In addition to the industrial sector, our company delivers projects in administrative buildings, offices, hotels and residential properties.

Our team combines technical expertise, precision and commitment to high quality in every project.
